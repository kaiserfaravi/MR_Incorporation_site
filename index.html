About Us

First conceived as a humble garment trading endeavour in 2003, the enterprise swiftly evolved, embracing the dynamic realm of freelance online advertising and marketing by early 2008, marked by its formal registration with Companies House in the United Kingdom. This burgeoning venture, rich with ambition, further broadened its horizons, delving into IT-enabled services and an array of other pursuits. By early 2023, the narrative reached a transformative juncture as all endeavours coalesced under the aegis of M R Incorporation Holding & Management Limited, a distinguished entity formally recognised by Companies House in both the United Kingdom and the United Arab Emirates, embodying the spirit of progress and unity.
