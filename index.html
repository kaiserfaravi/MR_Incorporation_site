First conceived as a humble garment trading endeavour in 2003, the enterprise swiftly evolved, embracing the dynamic realm of freelance online advertising and marketing by early 2008, marked by its formal registration with Companies House in the United Kingdom. This burgeoning venture, rich with ambition, further broadened its horizons, delving into IT-enabled services and an array of other pursuits. By early 2023, the narrative reached a transformative juncture as all endeavours coalesced under the aegis of M R Incorporation Holding & Management Limited, a distinguished entity formally recognised by Companies House in both the United Kingdom and the United Arab Emirates, embodying the spirit of progress and unity.

From its modest inception as a part-time endeavour born within the confines of a university dormitory, to the establishment of a registered office amidst the storied charm of historic Covent Garden, and now to the strategic relocation of its operational headquarters to the vibrant heart of Dubai, our journey is a tale of ambition and resilience. This evolution is a proud testament to our steadfast dedication to operational excellence, a resolute adherence to our core values, and an unyielding pursuit of excellence in every endeavour we undertake.
Over the span of 21 extraordinary years, M R Incorporation Holding & Management Limited has crafted a legacy imbued with innovation and enduring growth, marked by investments nearing £200 million across a rich and diverse tapestry of industries.
As we gaze towards the horizon, the company is set to embark on its next ambitious journey, committing approximately £250 million over the coming three years to expand its existing ventures. In addition, a further £200 million will be reserved for judiciously chosen new opportunities, embodying our resolute growth strategy and an enduring vision of sustained success and boundless progress.
